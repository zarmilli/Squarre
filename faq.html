What is Squarre?
Squarre is a smart agricultural investment and transaction platform by square Gold Group built to enourage agriculture development and financial literacy for everyday users. It allows you to grow your money by investing in real-world assets through Square Gold Farms and also send or receive money securely.
How does Squarre work?
You deposit money into your Squarre wallet, invest in categorized assets, and track your growth. When assets mature, your capital plus interest is automatically returned to your wallet.
How much can I deposit?
You can deposit as little as R1, and there's no maximum limit. However, investing larger amounts may unlock higher returns based on the asset category.
When do I get my money?
Returns are credited to your wallet as soon as an investment matures. You can then withdraw or reinvest them at any time.
How long do withdrawals take?
Withdrawals typically take 1–3 business days to reflect, depending on your bank. We’ll notify you as soon as it's processed.
Is Squarre legit?
Yes, Squarre is built and maintained by Square Gold Group, a registered business committed to transparency and user trust. All funds are protected by strict security protocols.
Who is Square Gold Group?
Square Gold Group is a diversified company based in South Africa. It includes agricultural, property, and tech divisions, all focused on building sustainable wealth and community impact.
